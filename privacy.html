Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Alchemy Elements ("the App") handles information in relation to your use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. No registration or account creation is required to use the App.

The App stores game progress data (discovered elements, achievements, settings) locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences and local storage. This data never leaves your device.

2. Third-Party Advertising

The App uses third-party advertising services (Google AdMob and Yandex Ads) to display advertisements. These services may collect certain data from your device in accordance with their own privacy policies, including device identifiers and usage data for the purpose of serving relevant ads.

Google AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Yandex Ads Privacy Policy: https://yandex.com/legal/confidential

3. Permissions

The App requests only the permissions necessary for its operation: internet access (for displaying advertisements) and access to audio settings (for muting game sounds).

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: o_ria@vk.com
